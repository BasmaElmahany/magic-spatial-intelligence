<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Futuristic Brutalism / Tech-Noir**
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw & Structural**: Emphasize the "infrastructure" aspect of the complex with bold, blocky layouts and exposed grid lines.
  2. **Data-Driven Aesthetics**: Use visual motifs that resemble data streams, topographic lines, and coordinate systems.
  3. **High Contrast & Precision**: Sharp edges, high contrast between background and content to convey precision and accuracy (surveying/satellite data).
  4. **Immersive Depth**: Use layering and parallax to create a sense of 3D space, reflecting "Spatial Intelligence".

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Deep Space Blue & Void Black**: Represents the "Remote Sensing" and "Space" aspect.
  - **Neon Cyan & Electric Purple**: Accents representing "AI" and "Innovation".
  - **Signal Orange**: For call-to-actions and alerts, mimicking industrial safety and high-visibility surveying gear.
  - *Reasoning*: Evokes a feeling of cutting-edge technology, serious data analysis, and a look into the future.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular / Bento Box**: Content is organized in distinct, card-like modules that fit together like a puzzle or a dashboard.
  - **Asymmetric Grids**: Breaking the standard 12-column grid to create dynamic, unexpected arrangements.
  - **Split-Screen Navigation**: Vertical navigation on the side (like a control panel) rather than a top bar.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Topographic Lines**: Subtle animated background patterns resembling elevation maps.
  - **Glitch Effects**: On hover states for buttons and images to suggest digital processing.
  - **Monospaced Headers**: Using code-like fonts for section titles to reinforce the technical nature.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Micro-Interactions**: Buttons that "click" mechanically, hover effects that reveal data points.
  - **Smooth Scrolling with Inertia**: Giving weight to the movement through the site.

  **Animation**:
  - **Reveal Animations**: Elements slide in with a "staggered" effect, like data loading on a screen.
  - **Continuous Motion**: Background elements (like the topographic lines) slowly moving to keep the page alive.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Space Mono* or *JetBrains Mono* (Technical, precise).
  - **Body**: *Inter* or *Roboto* (Clean, readable, neutral).
  - **Hierarchy**: Large, bold, uppercase headings contrasted with small, technical metadata labels.
</idea>
